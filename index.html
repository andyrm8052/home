From that time, I always wanted to become someone that one day would create one of those pieces of technologies and change the world. I really didn't have a plan in my high school years. I didn't know exactly what I wanted but knew I that technology was the area that interested me the most.

I want to be able and be a part of something big that will change the world in a good way. Everything is advancing, getting smarter and reaching new places; I want to be a part of that.
